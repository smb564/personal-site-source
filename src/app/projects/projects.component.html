Social Media Analytics Platform  [code][2018]

A social media analytics platform which has the ability to extract reviews for any place, restaurant or any other entity, identify aspects/topics discussed in those reviews, find the sentiment expressed towards each identified aspect and extract the deeper emotions expressed in those reviews, in general, is developed. Each of the subtasks is identified as research components and we have done extensive research under each subtask.

  • Used Technologies: PyTorch, Tensorflow, Python, Flask, Java, node.js
  • Used Concepts: Deep Learning models for NLP, IKASL algorithm, GSOM
