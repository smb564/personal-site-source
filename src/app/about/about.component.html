I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Purdue University. My current research interests lie at the intersection of compilers and machine learning. I'm interested in both compilers (or systems in general) for machine learning and utilizing machine learning techniques for compilers. I've recently started working with Prof. Tiark Rompf's group at Purdue CS. I am a firm believer in the Software 2.0 paradigm and aspire to become a part of the transformation.

Before commencing my graduate studies, I have completed my B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Moratuwa, Faculty of Engineering, Sri Lanka with a First Class Honors. During my undergrads, I have done a research internship at StatNLP lab, Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), under the supervision of Prof. Lu Wei. Upon graduation, before starting my graduate studies, I have worked as a Research Engineer at WSO2 which is one of the largest open-source middleware solutions companies in the world.
